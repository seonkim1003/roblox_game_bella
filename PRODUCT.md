# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Two people share a private Roblox birthday performance. Bella is the Star playing three tactile minigames, while a trusted Director runs the live show from a glanceable control surface on laptop, tablet, or phone.

## Product Purpose

Bella's Birthday Bash turns a short two-player session into a personalized live premiere. The Star should feel celebrated and playfully challenged. The Director should always understand the show state, Bella's momentum, and the next helpful action without losing the emotional pacing of the performance.

## Brand Personality

Cozy, playful, intimate. The experience is theatrical without becoming loud or impersonal, and polished without feeling like a generic operations dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid rage-game pressure, precision-platforming language, jump scares, sudden loud feedback, casino-like reward treatment, dense enterprise dashboards, and ornamental controls that compete with the live performance.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the live show legible at a glance.
2. Make every Director action helpful to the Star.
3. Let the interface support the performance instead of becoming the performance.
4. Preserve the warmth and personality of a handmade birthday gift.
5. Prefer calm recovery states over blame or alarm.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support touch, keyboard and mouse, and controller without requiring a virtual cursor. Keep critical state understandable without color alone. Provide visible muted-audio and reduced-motion controls with visual alternatives. Maintain readable contrast and safe-area-aware layouts across phone, tablet, and desktop.
